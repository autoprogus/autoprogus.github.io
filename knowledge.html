📱 How to use Knowledge on Android

Step 1: Fill structure

Enter Section, Topic, Chapter, Paragraph, Keywords, and main content. Green highlight indicates filled fields.

Step 2: Smart filtering

Use checkboxes to search in specific fields. Real-time results list shows matched entries.

Step 3: Save & organize

Tap “Save” to store entry, “New” to reset, “Delete” to remove selected entry. Everything persists locally.

  • 1 Download Knowledge APK and install on Android 9.0+ (allow installation from unknown sources).
  • 2 Launch the app, read and accept the License Agreement (shown once).
  • 3 Fill the left side: search filters, or start typing in Section / Topic / Chapter etc. Keyboard adapts for optimal input.
  • 4 Press “💾 Save” – entry gets timestamp and appears in the left list.
  • 5 Use the search bar and checkboxes to instantly filter entries, tap any entry to edit.
💡 Pro tip: Keywords field supports comma separated values. The app uses a unique debounce timer to optimize graph updates. All data is stored in /data/data/org.qtproject.example/files/entries.json – easy to backup.

📱 System requirements (Android)

🤖 OS: Android 9.0 (Pie) or higher (API 28+)
📱 Architecture: ARM64 (arm64-v8a) / ARMv7 (armeabi-v7a)
🧠 RAM: 3 GB minimum (4 GB recommended for large datasets)
💾 Storage: 150 MB free space + space for your entries (JSON)
📲 Screen: 5.0" or larger recommended, works on phones and tablets
⚙️ Permissions: Storage access (read/write entries.json for backups)
🌐 Internet: Not required – fully offline knowledge base
🎯 Input: Virtual keyboard with predictive text disabled for better accuracy
⚡ Optimized for Android: Supports immersive mode, automatic orientation changes, and touch-friendly interface. Built with Qt for seamless native performance.
